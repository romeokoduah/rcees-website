About RCEES

Africa's Centre of Excellence for Energy and Environment

Established in 2019 under the World Bank's Africa Centres of Excellence initiative, RCEES addresses critical energy and environmental challenges across sub-Saharan Africa through internationally competitive research and graduate training.

Based at the University of Energy and Natural Resources (UENR) in Sunyani, Bono Region, the Centre produces graduates equipped to drive development through environmentally sustainable approaches to energy, power systems, and resource management.

"Building human capital, fostering research innovation, and promoting international partnerships for Africa's sustainable energy future."
Prof. Samuel Gyamfi, Centre Director

Research Themes

Six Research Areas

Problem-oriented research across energy systems and environmental sustainability, from transitions and storage to climate, health, and resource management.

Energy Transitions

Pathways from fossil fuels to renewable energy systems, including policy frameworks, technology adoption, and socio-economic dimensions of clean energy shifts.

RenewablesPolicyJust Transition

Energy Storage

Battery technologies, pumped hydro, thermal storage, and hybrid systems for grid stability and off-grid reliability across sub-Saharan Africa.

BatteriesPumped HydroHybrid

Energy, Society and Environment

Social dimensions of energy access, community energy models, energy poverty, gender and equity in energy systems, and environmental trade-offs.

AccessEquityCommunity

Environment and Health

Air and water quality, pollution impacts on human health, occupational hazards in energy sectors, and environmental determinants of disease in Africa.

Air QualityWASHPollution

Environmental Management

Waste management, environmental impact assessment, natural resource governance, and regulatory instruments for sustainable development.

WasteEIAGovernance

Environment and Climate

Climate change impacts, adaptation strategies, resilience building, carbon accounting, and mitigation pathways for vulnerable ecosystems and communities.

AdaptationResilienceCarbon

Academic Programmes

Graduate Training in Energy & Environment

Internationally accredited postgraduate programmes combining rigorous coursework with applied research across sub-Saharan Africa.

Energy Policy and Sustainability
Policy analysis, regulatory frameworks, and sustainability governance in energy systems.
Energy Economics
Economic analysis of energy markets, pricing, investment, and transition modelling.
Environmental Engineering and Management
Environmental impact assessment, waste management, water resources, and pollution control.
All MSc programmes are 18-month full-time with a research component.
Sustainable Energy Engineering and Management
Advanced study of renewable technologies, system dynamics, grid integration, and clean fuel systems.
Energy Policy and Sustainability
Research-intensive programme in energy governance, sustainability transitions, and regulatory design.
Energy Economics
Research-led programme in energy market analysis, resource economics, and development finance.
MPhil programmes are 24-month research-intensive degrees with thesis requirement.
Sustainable Energy Engineering and Management
Doctoral research in clean energy technologies, transition modelling, and grid systems for emerging economies.
Energy Policy and Sustainability
Original research in energy governance, just transitions, and sustainability policy design.
Energy Economics
Advanced research in energy market dynamics, investment, and economic modelling of energy transitions.
Environmental Engineering and Management
Doctoral research in climate adaptation, environmental governance, green economy, and resource management.
PhD programmes are 3 to 4 year research degrees. Candidates work within RCEES research themes.
Design and Installation of Standalone Solar PV
Hands-on training in solar PV system sizing, component selection, installation, and commissioning for off-grid applications.
Energy Audit
Practical training in energy auditing methodology, data collection, analysis, and recommendations for buildings and facilities.
NEBOSH Certification
National Examination Board in Occupational Safety and Health certification for environmental and workplace safety management.
Professional Certification for Energy Auditors and Managers
Specialization tracks: SEMP (55 hrs, Energy Managers), SEAP (44 hrs, Energy Analysts), EAP (50 hrs, Energy Auditors). Certified by Energy Commission and GhIE. Available online and in-person.
Short courses run 1 to 4 weeks. Open to working professionals and external participants.
