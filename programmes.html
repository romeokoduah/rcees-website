Academic Programmes

Graduate Training in Energy & Environment

Internationally accredited postgraduate programmes combining rigorous coursework with applied research across sub-Saharan Africa.

MSc Programmes

Energy Policy and Sustainability
Policy analysis, regulatory frameworks, and sustainability governance in energy systems.
View details
Energy Economics
Economic analysis of energy markets, pricing, investment, and transition modelling.
View details
Environmental Engineering and Management
Environmental impact assessment, waste management, water resources, and pollution control.
View details
All MSc programmes are 18-month full-time with a research component.

MPhil Programmes

Sustainable Energy Engineering and Management
Advanced study of renewable technologies, system dynamics, grid integration, and clean fuel systems.
View details
Energy Policy and Sustainability
Research-intensive programme in energy governance, sustainability transitions, and regulatory design.
View details
Energy Economics
Research-led programme in energy market analysis, resource economics, and development finance.
View details
MPhil programmes are 24-month research-intensive degrees with thesis requirement.

PhD Programmes

Sustainable Energy Engineering and Management
Doctoral research in clean energy technologies, transition modelling, and grid systems for emerging economies.
View details
Energy Policy and Sustainability
Original research in energy governance, just transitions, and sustainability policy design.
View details
Energy Economics
Advanced research in energy market dynamics, investment, and economic modelling of energy transitions.
View details
Environmental Engineering and Management
Doctoral research in climate adaptation, environmental governance, green economy, and resource management.
View details
PhD programmes are 3 to 4 year research degrees. Candidates work within RCEES research themes.

Professional Short Courses

Design & Installation of Solar PV Systems
Hands-on training in solar PV design, component selection, installation, and commissioning for off-grid and grid-tied systems. 2–3 weeks · GHS 3,500–5,500.
View details
Energy Efficiency & Energy Audit Certification
Professional energy auditing methodology, data analysis, ECM development, and certified energy auditor certification. 2 weeks · GHS 3,000–4,500.
View details
Renewable Energy Project Analysis & Financing
Feasibility analysis, risk assessment, financial modelling, and green financing for renewable energy projects. 1–2 weeks · GHS 2,500–4,000.
View details
NEBOSH Occupational Health & Safety Certification
Globally recognised NEBOSH-accredited certification covering OHS legislation, risk assessment, and safety management systems. 2–4 weeks · GHS 6,000–9,000.
View details
Solar-Powered Irrigation Systems (SPIS)
Design, sizing, and deployment of solar irrigation systems for smallholder and commercial agriculture. 1–2 weeks · GHS 2,500–4,000.
View details
ISO 50001:2018 Energy Management System (EnMS)
Implementation of ISO 50001 EnMS framework, internal auditing, energy performance indicators, and certification preparation. 1 week · GHS 2,500–3,500.
View details
Short courses run 1 to 4 weeks. Open to working professionals, students, and external participants. Fees vary by course.

World Bank-funded Africa Centre of Excellence at UENR, advancing energy and environmental sustainability research across sub-Saharan Africa.
